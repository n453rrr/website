About Aldgate Recruitment

Aldgate Recruitment is a dedicated recruitment agency providing qualified and reliable staff for hospitality and residential cleaning services across London. Our core values are professionalism, efficiency, and commitment to excellence in staffing solutions.

We support a range of businesses including hotels, pubs, restaurants, and private residences by matching them with the right candidates. Whether it's temporary cover or permanent placements, our network of vetted staff ensures consistency and quality service.
